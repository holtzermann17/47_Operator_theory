proof that the convex hull of S is open if S is open


Let S be an open set in some topological vector space V. For any sequence of positive real numbers Λ=(λ1,…,λn) with ∑i=1nλi=1 define

SΛ={x∈V such that x=∑i=1nλisi for si∈S}.

Then since addition and scalar multiplication are both open maps, each SΛ is open. Finally, the convex hull is clearly just

⋃ΛSΛ,

which is therefore open.
