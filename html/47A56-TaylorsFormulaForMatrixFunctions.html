Taylor’s formula for matrix functions


Let p be a polynomial and suppose 𝐀 and 𝐁 commute, i.e. 𝐀𝐁=𝐁𝐀, then

p⁢(𝐀+𝐁)=∑k=0n1k!⁢p(k)⁢(𝐀)⁢𝐁k.

where n=deg⁡(p).
